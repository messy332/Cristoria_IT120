# Coffee Cup Images Guide

## Image Requirements

Place your coffee cup variety images in this folder with the following names:

### Required Images:
1. `turkish_coffee.png` - Turkish Coffee Cup
2. `matcha_cup.png` - Japanese Matcha Cup
3. `vietnam_egg.png` - Vietnam Egg Coffee Cup
4. `espresso_demitasse.png` - Espresso Demitasse Cup
5. `double_walled.png` - Double-Walled Glass Cup
6. `stainless_steel.png` - Reusable Stainless Steel Cup
7. `cappuccino.png` - Cappuccino Cup
8. `latte_glass.png` - Latte Glass Cup
9. `yixing_clay.png` - Yixing Clay Cup
10. `ceramic_pourover.png` - Ceramic Pour-over Cup

## Image Specifications:
- **Format**: PNG or JPG
- **Recommended Size**: 800x800 pixels minimum
- **Aspect Ratio**: Square (1:1) preferred
- **Background**: Clean, preferably white or transparent
- **Quality**: High resolution for clear display

## Tips for Best Results:
- Use high-quality product photos
- Ensure good lighting and clear focus
- Show the cup from a recognizable angle
- Include the full cup in the frame
- Avoid cluttered backgrounds
